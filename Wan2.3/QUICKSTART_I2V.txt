  2. Use 480P: --size 480*832 --sample_shift 3.0
  3. Add more GPUs: --nproc_per_node=4

Problem: Slow generation
Solution:
  1. Use 4 GPUs instead of 2
  2. Reduce steps: --sample_steps 30
  3. Use 480P resolution

Problem: Port conflict with S2V
Solution: Already handled! I2V uses port 12346, S2V uses 12345
